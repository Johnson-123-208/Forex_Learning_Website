# FOREX LEARNING APP - MASTER CONTENT GENERATION PROMPTS

Detailed below are three master prompts designed to generate expert-level, high-fidelity curriculum data for your trading platform. 

Instructions for usage:
1. Use an advanced LLM (Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o).
2. Paste ONE prompt at a time.
3. If the AI cuts off, type "Continue exactly from where you left off".
4. Copy the resulting JavaScript code directly into your `src/data/modules-full.js` file.
